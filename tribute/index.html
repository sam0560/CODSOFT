About

Cristiano Ronaldo, born February 5, 1985, is a legendary Portuguese footballer. Starting at Sporting CP, he moved to Manchester United in 2003, winning multiple titles. In 2009, he joined Real Madrid, becoming their top scorer and winning four Champions League titles and Ballon d'Or awards. After playing for Juventus and returning to Manchester United, he now plays for Al Nassr in Saudi Arabia. Renowned for his goal-scoring and versatility, Ronaldo is also a global sports icon and philanthropist.
